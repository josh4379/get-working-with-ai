The problem

Most AI rollouts stall in the evaluation phase

Six months of pilots, a dozen tools nobody agreed on, a bill nobody can explain, and a team still pasting work into a free chatbot. The blocker is almost never the technology — it's the absence of a decision, a budget guardrail, and a plan.

We turn “we should use AI” into a setup your team actually uses

One partner, one fixed price, one decision to make. We do the choosing, configuring, and training — you get a governed setup your finance team and your staff can both live with.

It starts with a one-to-one call on Teams or Zoom. We map what your people actually do all day, where AI genuinely helps, and — just as importantly — where it doesn't. Then we recommend a provider, pick models that fit the work, and set hard spending limits before a single licence is bought.

Within 30 days you're live: admin portal configured, settings tuned, optional SSO so nobody manages another password, an implementation plan written for your organisation, and a plain-English guide that shows employees how to get real work done without burning budget.

What's Included

Nine things, all of them done for you

This is the full scope of the Implementation Package — kickoff to trained, governed rollout. Nothing here is an upsell, and nothing is left on your desk. The Clarity Package covers the first three as advice, with a written path forward.

Kickoff session

A one-to-one session on Teams or Zoom, booked as soon as you check out. We learn how your team works, what's sensitive, and what success looks like — before anyone touches a provider.

The provider decision, made

A straight recommendation on which provider fits your organisation, and why. No vendor kickbacks, no hype, no twelve-column comparison matrix for you to interpret.

The right model for each job

Heavy models for hard work, cheap ones for the routine. Most overspending comes from using a premium model to reformat a spreadsheet — we set that straight up front.

Spending limits that hold

Our headline feature. Hard caps configured at the provider, so usage can grow across the business without anyone opening an invoice they can't explain.

A rollout plan for your org

Built with your team, not delivered to them: which workflows change, in what order, and who owns each one.

Admin portal, configured

Roles, permissions, retention and data settings configured to match the policies you already have — so governance isn't a thing you retrofit later.

SSO for employees

Optional single sign-on, so access follows your existing joiners-and-leavers process. When someone leaves, their access leaves with them.

A guide your staff will read

Short, practical, and specific to your setup. Real prompts for real tasks your people do — which is the difference between adoption and a licence nobody opens twice.

FAQ

The questions we get asked most

Straight answers on scope, timeline, cost, and what happens if it doesn't suit you. Anything else, email us before you buy.

What is included in the Clarity Package?

A one-to-one deep-dive on Teams or Zoom, an honest assessment of where AI helps and where it won't, a provider and model direction, the cost and risk traps to avoid, a recommended path forward, and a written summary you can forward to stakeholders without editing it first.

What is included in the Implementation Package?

Everything in Clarity, plus the build: provider chosen and configured, models matched to jobs, hard spending limits set, admin portal and settings done, optional SSO, a rollout plan written with your team, and a how-to guide for staff — live in 30 days or less.

How long does it take?

Thirty days from kickoff to live, at the outside — and often less. The deadline is the point: it's what stops an AI project turning into an eighteen-month evaluation that quietly gets shelved.

Do we need AI expertise in-house?

None. That's the entire premise. We handle provider selection, model choices, admin configuration, spending controls, and staff enablement — and we hand back a setup your existing IT team can run without us.

How do you stop the bill running away?

Two ways. We match the right model to each job — most overspending is a premium model doing trivial work — and we set hard limits at the provider so there's a ceiling, not just a dashboard you'd have to remember to check.

Which package should we start with?

Clarity if the decision isn't made or budget isn't approved. Implementation if you're ready to go. Buying Clarity first and Implementation later is a normal path — the discovery work carries over rather than being repeated.

What does it cost?

Both packages are a fixed fee, quoted in writing before any work starts — no hourly rates, no variable scope. Provider licence fees are paid directly to your provider and sit outside our fee; we size those with you so there are no surprises on either side.

We already have some AI licences. Is it too late?

No — it's the most common place we start. Scattered licences, no admin controls, and spend nobody can account for is a normal starting point. We audit what you already have, keep what's working, and fold the rest into one governed setup.

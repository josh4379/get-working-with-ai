FAQ

AI implementation FAQ

Common questions about the Clarity Package, Implementation Package, timeline, and cost control.

What is included in the Clarity Package?

A one-to-one Teams or Zoom deep-dive into your needs, assessment of where AI can help, high-level provider and model direction, early cost-control and risk guidance, recommended next steps, and a written summary you can share with stakeholders.

What is included in the Implementation Package?

Discovery call, AI provider setup within 30 days or less, organisation implementation plan, provider and model selection, cost control with spending limits, admin portal setup, optional SSO, and an employee how-to guide for efficient AI use.

How long does AI implementation take?

The Implementation Package is designed to have you live with an AI provider within 30 days or less from kickoff — without months of stalled evaluation.

Do we need AI experts on our team?

No. We handle provider selection, model guidance, admin configuration, cost controls, and employee enablement so you can implement AI stress free — without needing to be experts yourselves.

How do you control AI spending?

Cost control is our headline feature. We advise on the right models for the right jobs and set spending limits with your AI provider so usage stays efficient and budgets stay predictable.

Should we start with Clarity or Implementation?

Choose the Clarity Package if you need direction or stakeholder buy-in first. Choose the Implementation Package if you are ready to roll out now. Many clients do Clarity first, then Implementation.
